How do I get paid out, and how is it calculated?

TIERED PRICING

Cx Collective, Inc will charge you a Service Fee based on the total Sales Revenue collected on your behalf each month (less any refunds or reversals) for the duration of your relationship with your Client (the “Engagement Relationship”).

The Service Fee will be deducted from your payment account prior to your sales revenue being deposited into your authorized bank account.

The Service Fee rates decrease as the total Sales Revenue collected on your behalf meet certain thresholds as follows (“Tiered Pricing”)

SERVICE FEE RATES:
$1,000.00 and below - 20%
$1,000.01 to $10,000.00 - 10%
$10,000.01 and up - 5%

Marketplace Returns

How are returns and exchanges processed?

Free Standard USPS shipping + returns for all orders conducted on the Cx Marketplace. No limit on number of orders or returns.

Items shipped from Cx can be returned within 30 days of receipt of shipment in most cases. Some products have different policies or requirements associated with them. All items must be unused, unworn, and in returnable condition. Items that have been resized, damaged, or otherwise altered after delivery won't be accepted for return. Ensure you enclose the correct and complete item when making a return. The correct item must be returned to receive a refund. To protect our employees and customers, Cx disposes of incorrect/mistaken returns. Once the item is received at our fulfillment center, it takes 2 business days for the refund to be processed and 7-15 business days for the refund amount to show up in your account.

Expert Advice

How do the agricultural labs work?

Our agricultural lab is 10,000 sq ft, with 15-spaces that can be rented by a cycle of three months to one year or longer. Cx Agricultural Labs are customizable, and are optimized to grow cannabis or other agricultural products. In addition to that Cx Agricultural Labs are able to accommodate a farmer’s preference for either soil or hydroponic systems. Each lab includes a separate space for cultivating seedlings, and clones to maximize production. 

Security is very important to us, and our collective members- using Enterprise level technology, you’re able to always have eyes on your crop, know and adjust your environmental settings (temperature, PM 2.5, TVOC, and humidity).


With our intuitive, cloud based security platform- You’ll have all access in the palm of your hand! 

What resources and technology is featured in the agricultural labs?

Our agricultural labs feature a full suite of technology and tools for growing and processing cannabis from start to finish. These include, but not limited to automated lighting, watering, and nutrient delivery systems, full-spectrum customizable LED lighting, full environmental monitoring sensors, mini-split air circulation, flash freezers, trimmers, and final processing tools for production. If you require any specific tools not featured here; please contact our Account Manager, and we’ll love to collaborate to fulfill your specific needs that can also serve the collective.

Taxes We Collect and Remit from Buyers on Behalf of the Seller, United States Sales Tax.
