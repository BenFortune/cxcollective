In Good Company

Our goal is to enable the artisan, farmer, proprietor who may be currently running their business out of a home office the opportunity to directly connect with their community; in person and online, with a larger audience than they currently have access to.

Each member of our collective is hand selected and is tasked with upholding the values of Cx day in and day out.

Our founders come from a diverse set of backgrounds and cultures. We believe that inclusion matters because diversity is at the core of who we are. We do not tolerate hate and we stand with our friends and neighbors from traditionally marginalized communities.

This is our commitment to our employees, clients and the communities we live in around the world.
