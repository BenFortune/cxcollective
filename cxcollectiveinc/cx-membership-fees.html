Transparent Membership Fees

All members of Cx Collective will contribute to the Cx Collective Annual Fees. Annual Fees are your general collective membership and contribute to access to enterprise technology and additional resources. In addition, all Cx Collective Members will have access to the Cx Virtual Community (so long as they follow the community guidelines).

Cx Marketplace, Tiered Pricing: Cx Collective, Inc will charge you a Service Fee based on the total Sales Revenue collected on your behalf each month (less any refunds or reversals) for the duration of your relationship with your Client (the “Engagement Relationship”). The Service Fee will be deducted from your payment account prior to your sales revenue being deposited into your authorized bank account. In some states, we will collect the sales tax amount from the buyer and pay this to the relevant tax authority. The sales tax amount will appear in your payment account and is deducted from your current balance. For items shipped to certain states, including but not limited to Washington state (or, for digital items, those purchased with a Washington state billing address), we will use the category you assigned to each listing to calculate, collect, and remit the applicable sales tax. Note that in those states where we are required to collect on sales made by nonresident sellers, we will also be collecting on those sales made by resident sellers. As an example, we will be collecting on all (taxable) purchases made in Minnesota, including those purchases made by Minnesota buyers from Minnesota sellers. The Service Fee rates decrease as the total Annual Sales Revenue collected on your behalf meet certain thresholds as follows (“Tiered Pricing ”):

SERVICE FEE RATES
$1,000.00 and below - 20%
$1,000.01 to $10,000.00 - 10%
$10,000.01 and up - 5%
Cx Merchandising, Storage, and Fulfillment

Merchandising: Cx Merchandising will merchandise and or assist in the merchandising of any product sent to our warehouse (this cost does not include the cost of storage which is billed separately (per item per day). If your organization only requires assistance with Merchandising a product you will be charged for shipping from the Cx Merchandising Offices and or Location(s). Should you require Merchandising, Storage, and Fulfillment you will not be charged additional shipping fees. Merchandising for any product is $0.01/ Item/ Placement. Therefore, if you have one item with three individual placements your total cost for Merchandising would total $0.03/ item. Cx Merchandising is not responsible for the printing costs or fees of any labels or materials. Cx Collective Member is responsible for any costs or additional materials required on their behalf.

Storage: Cx Storage is simple $0.01/Item/ Day. Therefore if you have a total of 60 items to be stored in one thirty day period, your total storage fee would calculate to $18 = ((60*($0.01))*30).

Fulfillment, Tiered Pricing: Cx Collective, Inc will charge you a Service Fee based on the total  Annual Order Volume conducted on Cx Marketplace or through Cx Order Fulfillment technology that requires a Cx Collective member to fulfill your order on your behalf for the duration of your relationship with your Client (the “Engagement Relationship”). This Service Fee will be deducted from your payment account prior to your sales revenue being deposited into your authorized bank account or auto billed on the 31st of each month if your organization is not listed on Cx Marketplace. The Service Fee decrease as the total Annual Order Volume meets certain thresholds as follows (“Tiered Pricing ”):

SERVICE FEE RATES
0 - 200 Orders/ Year - $1.75/ Shipment
201 - 500 Orders/ Year - $1.25/ Shipment
501+ Orders/ Year - $0.75/ Shipment

When possible all payments for Cx Merchandising, Storage, and Fulfillment is collected on one invoice on the first day of the following calendar month for all applicable activation types (for example, if your organization does Cx Merchandising, Storage, and Fulfillment - and your billing period is September 1 - 30, your organization will be automatically charged on October 1). If your organization is only participating in Cx Merchandising 50% deposit will be due before the project is started and 50% is due upon completion. Cx Storage and Fulfillment will always be billed based on usage of the previous month.

Cx Agriculture Lab, CoWorking Spaces, and Virtual Mailboxes pricing is based on location and size required by the Cx Collective Member. All agreements for each of these activations are month to month unless otherwise stated in a Collective Members contract agreement. Please see your lease agreement for a Cx Agricultural Lab, Co Working Space, and or Virtual Mailbox for more information.
