The Cx Collective Member, Service Level Agreement

With Cx you're not getting "another co-op" - you're getting a community that's committed to your success as an entrepreneur and your businesses success.

Cx Collective Inc has developed and owns and offers a World Wide Web (“Web”) Application service, Virtual Community Platform, Retail Marketplace, Fulfillment Services, Office Spaces, and Agricultural Labs that provide(s) Customer with a license to use certain functions of the Software to access an “online” version of the Software through a designated Web Site (the “Service). In order to use said service a Collective Member, Company, and or Client agrees that it must purchase a Cx Collective Membership and or Service. Additional information regarding a specific Membership should be directed towards our Collective Member Advisory Team. Please note that without proper confirmation of a Collective Members Account our Collective Advisory Team cannot and will not release any information to you about a specific membership as Cx Collective values the privacy of all of its members.

All Collective Members are assigned and Advisory Team member as their main point of contact; however, if you are unable to reach him/ her/ they you can always trust that you will receive Top Notch Customer Support. “Customer Support” includes routine answers to questions, error correction, and maintenance modifications requested by the Customer via telephone, email, or a messaging system within the platform and provided during Cx Collective's normal business hours. We will always do our best to respond to any Cx Collective Member within a timely fashion (within 2 - 6 business hours). We strive to go above and beyond for our Collective Members and will do our very best to assist as best as we can. However, Customer Support does not include programming, detailed or specialized maintenance, provision of enhancements, or support different in kind or amount from that which is provided to other customers of the Service, including, without limitation, problems that may arise in interfacing or operating the Service or any of its components with non-supported, unusual or proprietary systems.

Any Enhancements made to Cx Collective Web Service will always be included at no additional cost to our Active Collective Members (Active Collective Members, are defined as currently using a service of Cx Collective).

Cx is committed to its impact on the communities where our members are active and as such will always strive to help our members create a positive impact on their local economies.
